Review Ratings Classifier

We developed a tool that classifies customer reviews into ratings using a machine learning model. We built the model using Logistic Regression from Scikit-Learn and used it to analyze the Amazon Fine Food reviews dataset. The reviews are rated on a scale of 1-5, where a review rated as 1-star is the worst review and a 5-star rated review is the best. As of the most recent publication we only recommend entering food reviews.

Amazon Fine Foods Review Ratings

We built a machine learning model to help predict the ratings for Amazon Fine Foods reviews. The Reviews are rated on a scale of 1-5, where 1 means the worst review and 5 means the best review.



Models and Analysis

We built a Sentiment Analysis (SA) model using LogisticRegression from SKLearn, and a Principal Component Analysis (PCA) model that also utilized SKLearn. After working with both we realized that the SA model suited our needs better than the PCA model did.
